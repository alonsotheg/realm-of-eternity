# Realm of Eternity: Game Reference

This document provides comprehensive information about mobs, drops, skills, and systems in the game.

---

## Skills

### Combat Skills
- **Strength** - Increases attack power (+20 per level)
- **Attack** - Increases attack power (+10 per level)
- **Defense** - Reduces incoming damage (2% per level, capped at 50%)
- **Health** - Increases max HP (+100 per level)

### Gathering Skills
- **Mining** - Gather ores from rocks (Copper, Coal, Iron)
- **Woodcutting** - Chop trees for wood (+ seed drops)
- **Fishing** - Catch fish (Shrimp, Trout, Salmon)
- **Herblore** - Gather herbs and craft potions (+ seed drops)
- **Farming** - Grow crops at your home for food

### Production Skills
- **Cooking** - Cook raw fish into food (heals HP)
- **Smithing** - Craft weapons and armor from ores
- **Herblore** - Craft healing potions and buff potions

## Home System

### Overview
Build homes at any location to unlock farming plots and storage.

### Home Tiers
| Tier | Total Level Required | Wood Cost | Farm Plots | Storage Slots |
|------|---------------------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| 1    | 25                  | 2,000     | 50         | 25            |
| 2    | 50                  | 5,000     | 100        | 50            |
| 3    | 100                 | 10,000    | 250        | 75            |

### Home Features
- **One home per location** - Build homes at different locations
- **Permanent** - Homes cannot be destroyed, only upgraded
- **Storage** - Store up to X unique item types (unlimited quantity per type)
- **Farming** - Plant seeds to grow consumable crops

## Farming System

### Overview
Farming is unlocked by building a home. Plant seeds in plots to grow crops.

### Crops & Growth Times
| Seed | Crop | Growth Time | Farming Level | Harvest XP | Heal Amount |
|------|------|-------------|---------------|------------|-------------|
| Strawberry Seed | Strawberry | 10 min | 1 | 15 | 150 HP |
| Blueberry Seed | Blueberry | 10 min | 1 | 15 | 150 HP |
| Raspberry Seed | Raspberry | 15 min | 5 | 25 | 250 HP |
| Blackberry Seed | Blackberry | 15 min | 5 | 25 | 250 HP |
| Lettuce Seed | Lettuce | 20 min | 10 | 40 | 350 HP |
| Radish Seed | Radish | 25 min | 15 | 55 | 450 HP |
| Tomato Seed | Tomato | 40 min | 25 | 85 | 650 HP |
| Cucumber Seed | Cucumber | 40 min | 25 | 85 | 650 HP |
| Squash Seed | Squash | 90 min | 40 | 150 | 1200 HP |

### Farming Mechanics
- **Offline Growth** - Crops continue growing while you're away
- **Growth Pauses** - Ready crops wait until harvested (no auto-replant)
- **XP on Harvest** - Gain Farming XP only when harvesting
- **Consumable Crops** - All harvested crops can be eaten for HP
- **Quick Plant** - Bulk plant all seeds of one type with a single click
- **Harvest All** - Harvest all ready crops at once

### Seed Sources
Seeds drop from:
- **Woodcutting** - All tree types drop seeds (10-12% chance, 1-2 seeds)
- **Herblore** - Herb gathering drops seeds (8-12% chance, 1-2 seeds)
- **Enemies** - Various mobs drop seeds (see drop tables)
- **Chests** - All chest types contain seeds (see Loot Chests section)

## Map Navigation & Travel System

### Overview
Travel between locations using an interactive island map. Locations are connected based on proximity and accessibility.

### Location Connections
- **Starter Village** connects to: Forest, Cave, Fishing Pond, Blacksmith
- **Blacksmith** connects to: Starter Village
- **Forest** connects to: Starter Village, Mountains
- **Cave** connects to: Starter Village, Volcanic Plain
- **Fishing Pond** connects to: Starter Village, Volcanic Plain
- **Volcanic Plain** connects to: Cave, Fishing Pond, Mountains
- **Mountains** connects to: Forest, Volcanic Plain

### Travel Mechanics
- **Travel Time**: Fixed 8 seconds regardless of distance
- **No Interruption**: Travel cannot be canceled once started
- **Offline Travel**: Travel completes even if you close the game
- **Activity Pause**: All gathering and combat stops during travel
- **Scene Display**: Shows traveling animation with progress bar

### Map Features
- **Current Location**: Highlighted in blue
- **Adjacent Locations**: Available for travel (green)
- **Distant Locations**: Locked until connected paths are unlocked
- **Visual Connections**: Lines show available travel routes

## Auto-Eat System

Configure automatic food consumption during combat:
- Set HP threshold (default: 60%)
- Select food type (Shrimp, Trout, Salmon, Frog Legs, or Potions)
- Automatically consumes food when HP drops below threshold
- Works with both regular food and healing potions

---

## Combat Mechanics

### Attack Calculation
- Base Attack = Strength Level × 20 + Attack Level × 10
- Equipment bonuses add to attack
- Potion bonuses apply (flat + percentage)

### Defense Calculation
- Base Damage Reduction = Defense Level × 2% (capped at 50%)
- Additional reduction from equipment defense stat
- Potion bonuses can increase reduction (capped at 75%)

### Experience Distribution
- Combat skill (Strength/Attack): Full EXP
- Health skill: 1/3 of combat EXP
- Potion XP bonuses apply to all gained EXP

---

## Gathering Mechanics

### Skill Speed Modifiers
- Tool bonuses reduce gathering time (e.g., Bronze Pickaxe: -10%)
- Haste potions reduce gathering time (10-25%)
- Bonuses stack multiplicatively

### Resource Drops
- Each resource has a base drop rate
- Extra resource potions give 15% chance for +1 bonus
- Rare drops possible from gathering (chests, gems, seeds)

### Seed Drops from Gathering
**Woodcutting:**
- Birch Tree: Strawberry Seed (12%, 1-2), Blueberry Seed (12%, 1-2)
- Oak Tree: Raspberry Seed (12%, 1-2), Blackberry Seed (12%, 1-2), Lettuce Seed (10%, 1-2)
- Willow Tree: Radish Seed (10%, 1-2), Tomato Seed (10%, 1-2), Cucumber Seed (10%, 1-2), Squash Seed (8%, 1-2)

**Herblore:**
- Greenleaf/Swiftgrass: Strawberry Seed (12%, 1-2), Blueberry Seed (12%, 1-2)
- Earthweed: Raspberry Seed (12%, 1-2), Lettuce Seed (10%, 1-2)
- Bloodroot: Blackberry Seed (12%, 1-2), Radish Seed (10%, 1-2)
- Glowcap: Tomato Seed (10%, 1-2), Cucumber Seed (10%, 1-2)
- Ironleaf: Tomato Seed (10%, 1-2), Cucumber Seed (10%, 1-2)
- Silverleaf/Emberleaf: Squash Seed (10%, 1-2)
- Nightshade Bloom: Squash Seed (8%, 1-2)

### Cooking
- Consumes 1 raw food + 1 wood per cook
- Random yield: 1-3 cooked items per action
- Higher yield when more resources available
- Auto-repeats until resources run out

## Potion Effects

### Active Buff System
- Only one potion per category can be active
- Buffs persist during AFK (timer continues)
- Effects shown in Active Buffs panel
- Automatically expire when duration ends

### Effect Categories
- **Attack** - Increases attack power
- **Defense** - Increases defense/damage reduction
- **Hybrid** - Multiple combat stats
- **Survival** - Max HP buffs
- **Skill Speed** - Faster gathering
- **Skill XP** - Increased XP gains
- **Skill Efficiency** - Extra resources
- **Loot** - Better drop rates
- **Utility** - AFK time, interrupts
